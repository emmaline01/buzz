About Buzz

There are various types of bullying that are affecting kids in Cupertino, such as cyber bullying, physical bullying, social bullying and verbal bullying. All types of bullying negatively affect the bullies, the bullied, and the bystanders. Mental health and substance use are both linked to bullying. The best thing to do is seek out help from parents, teachers, or counselors, but 70-80% of students do not feel comfortable openly talking about their issues. Our website is made to help students get the help they need anonymously. Just sign up for a free account to get started!


About Us

We are a group of middle and high school students happy to help the residents of Cupertino, especially our fellow students, with any bullying problems. This website was created to inform students about bullying and encourage students to anonymously eceive aid from their schools to combat bullying.


About the Counselors

The counselors on this website are from various schools around Cupertino. When you sign up you can send emails to any of the counselors, so you do not have to limit yourself to the counselors from at your school.
